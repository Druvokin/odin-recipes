Adobo

Description

Adobo is the national dish of the Philippines, which consists of chunks of meat, fish, or vegetables, marinated in vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, and spices and then stewed in the marinade.

Ingredients

  • vegetable oil
  • chicken
  • onion
  • minced garlic
  • soy sauce
  • white vinegar
  • black pepper
  • bay leaf
  • sugar
  • chicken knorr cube
